# RabbitHole

Walkthrough: RabbitHole - A Calm, Reflective Facilitator
RabbitHole is now ready to help you climb from confusion to clarity. The app acts as a facilitative guide for difficult conversations, using a 4-stage vertical process.

## Key Features

Vertical Progress Path: A visual metaphor of climbing out of a rabbit hole.
Facilitative AI: Powered by Gemini, the AI asks reflective questions one at a time and never writes scripts for you.

Dynamic Theme Detection: Provide a detailed description upfront, and the AI will identify themes to start your climb.

Alice in Wonderland Aesthetic: Soft colors, glassmorphism, and minimal design.

## How to Run

Backend:

Ensure your Gemini API key is in

Frontend:

## The 4 Stages

1. Preparation
Clarifies your thoughts and emotions. If your initial input is short, the facilitator will ask follow-up questions about the impact and core story.

2. Opening with Empathy
Helps you understand the other person's perspective and draft an opening that reflects your true intention.

3. Active Listening
Prepares you for triggers and listening strategies (paraphrasing, clarifying questions).

4. Collaborative Problem Solving
Frames the issue as a shared challenge and identifies potential solutions.

Final Screen: Conversation Compass
Once you reach the surface, you'll receive a summary of your reflections to guide your actual conversation.

IMPORTANT

The AI is constrained to act only as a facilitator. It will never tell you what to say or simulate the other person.

TIP

Use the "Finish the climb" button once you feel you've gained sufficient clarity in each stage.
